History of Pig

Pig is a folk game. It has been played and passed down for so long that the origin is unknown. It was first written and published about in 1945 by John Scarne.

There have been many commercial variants of Pig over the years.

Some notable commercial versions include:

  • Pass the Pigs
  • Snout!
  • Pig Dice
  • Skunk
