Strategy

Since mathematics professors use Pig to teach probability, the game has been very thoroughly analyzed and optimized.

The Gettysburg College Department of Computer Science has a visual and informative analysis.

The general rule for an optimal player is to hold at 20. If your turn total is less than 20, the odds are in your favor and you should continue rolling.

This "hold at 20" rule breaks down near the end of the game though. If your opponent has a score of 99, and you have 75, it's best to continue rolling beyond 20, since your opponent is statistically likely to win on his/her next turn.
