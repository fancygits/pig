Welcome to the Pigpen

Pig is a simple dice game, a game of chance and strategy. You must be the first player to score over 100, but be careful! If you are a greedy piggy, you will lose all those points!

Pig is what's known as a jeopardy dice game. All the points accumulated during your turn are in jeopardy, you risk losing them.

So should you roll the dice and risk losing it all? Or should you hold on to your points and risk letting your opponents win?

Grab a die and some friends (and probably a score sheet) and find out if you will be the big pig in the pen!
